Website Development Workflow

Here are steps involved in the development of a website

STEP 1

Requirement Gathering

You tell your vision and we make notes ;) First step is to listen to your great plan, discuss things and ask you for all we need from you.  

STEP 2

Planning

Once we know your vision, it's time for us to brainstorm and apply the best solution for your website.

STEP 3

Design & Development

At this point our experts will be busy with nuts-and-bolts making your website. From how it looks to how it works is all done here. We make sure everything is perfect before we send it to you for review.

STEP 4

Review & Changes

This is time for you to have a look at the websIte and give your feedback. Once review is done, your requested changes would be made. 

STEP 5

Project Sign off

Finally the time to finalize payment and deliver you the completed project. You’ll have full access to your website and out support team to ensure you’re continually happy with your site.

STEP 6

Live Website

Yes! The time to let the world know of your presence. You can finally share it with whomever you want, add link in your email signatures and what not. 

Step 7

SEO & Promotion

Website alone might not be enough, Google is probably your best friend in reaching potential clients. Time to get indexed in Google and reach those clients.
