FAQs

Are domain & SSL really free?

Yes! When you buy multiple years of hosting service, we offer free domain registration with that. For SSL, all the accounts get that for free.

How much hosting space do I need?

That depends upon the type of the website and the content you have on the website. If it's just a static website with few pages, the Essential package should be enough. Maybe a WordPress website with few 100 pages? Better sign up for Business package. You can always upgrade or downgrade.

Which domain extension is good for me?

If your reach is international and you want people from around the world to visit your website, you might want to go with the popular .com or .net (gTLD) options. If your business is not that extensive and you want to promote your services within your province or country, you can pick your country's extension .ca or .in (ccTLD).
