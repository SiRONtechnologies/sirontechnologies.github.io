FAQs

How is eCommerce site different from any website?

eCommerce website requires a different type of setup in order to accept and complete the orders. Plus you might want to recieve online payments too! That all makes eCommerce setup different from any other type of website.

What is a payment gateway?

A payment gateway is something that processes all the financial transactions online. When someone places the order, payment gateway takes that amount and send it to your bank account.

What payment gateway would I need?

That depends upon your location. For example Canada has different payments gateways than Germany, so you might want to check which ones are available in your country. Paypal is one of the most popular payment gateways, and it's most likely available in your country too.
